NHS Hack Day 3: Oxford

{% page_type %} {# Posted by David Miller at 18:47 GMT on 12/29/2012 #}

The 3rd NHS Hack Day takes place in Tingewick Hall, John Radcliffe Hospital, Oxford, on the 26th and 27th of January 2013. Be there!

Problems and datasets

Submit a problem, idea, or dataset you have and we can help build a solution and put you in touch with top software developers.

Solutions and code

NHS Hack Day (software development day) is committed to producing high quality software that improves health. That's why we support openness and invite you to share code with us on github.
